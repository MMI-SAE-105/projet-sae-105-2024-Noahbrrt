About

Hi, I’m Noah Barret, and I’m a first-year student in the MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) program in Montbéliard.

This website is a project centered around the theme “Prehistory of the Future.”

The idea of the "Prehistory of the Future" is intriguing, combining reflection on the distant past with anticipation of the future. It examines how today's knowledge and technologies might be perceived in a past era, much like how we perceive prehistoric artifacts today.

Feel free to join me on social networks, accessible at the bottom of this page.

Glossary

Bochiball: A fictional sport created by a tribe after discovering a football. Two teams compete to pass the ball through a hoop while playing barefoot on uneven terrain.

FIFA (Fédération Internationale de Football Association): The international organization founded in 1904 that governs world football, organizes major international competitions like the FIFA World Cup, and establishes the official rules of the game.
